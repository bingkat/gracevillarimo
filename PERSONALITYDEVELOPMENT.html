PERSONALITY QUALITIES DEVELOP DURING OJT EXPERIENCE

Being Responsible

I was given a variety of jobs during my OJT, all of which required a high level of responsibility. I made sure to finish my tasks on time, adhere to deadlines, and produce high-quality work. I assumed responsibility for my duties and made care to swiftly convey any difficulties or problems.

Having Self-confidence

In order to excel in my OJT, I needed to have confidence in my abilities and knowledge. I actively participated in team discussions, shared my ideas, and contributed to problem-solving. By demonstrating confidence, I was able to gain the trust of my colleagues, which led to more significant responsibilities and opportunities.

Possessing Appropriate Social Skills
Working in a professional environment requires strong interpersonal skills. I made an effort to build positive relationships with my colleagues and superiors by actively listening, being respectful, and communicating effectively. I engaged in open and constructive dialogue, which helped in establishing a collaborative work environment.

Being Honest

Honesty is crucial in any work setting. During my OJT, I always maintained honesty and integrity in my interactions. I provided accurate and transparent information, admitted mistakes when they occurred, and took responsibility for resolving any issues that arose.

Having Integrity

Integrity is an essential quality in the workplace, and I made sure to uphold it during my OJT. I consistently demonstrated ethical behavior, followed company policies and procedures, and handled sensitive information with utmost confidentiality and professionalism.

Demonstrating Adaptability and Flexibility

In the dynamic and fast-paced environment of my OJT, I encountered various challenges and changes. I embraced these situations with adaptability and flexibility. I quickly adjusted to new tasks, technologies, and team dynamics, allowing me to contribute effectively to the organization's goals.

Being a Team Player

Collaboration and teamwork were vital during my OJT experience. I actively participated in group projects, supported my team members, and contributed my skills and knowledge to achieve common objectives. I actively sought feedback and incorporated it into my work, fostering a positive and cooperative work environment.

Being Punctual and Efficient

I recognized the importance of time management and being punctual during my OJT. I always arrived on time for meetings, completed tasks within given deadlines, and prioritized my work effectively. By demonstrating punctuality and efficiency, I gained a reputation for reliability and professionalism.

Being Self-directed

Troughout my OJT, I developed a sense of self-direction by taking initiative and being proactive. I sought out opportunities to learn and grow, conducted independent research, and suggested improvements to existing processes. By being self-directed, I was able to contribute more effectively to the team's goals.

Possessing a Positive Work Attitude

For both personal and professional development, it is essential to have a cheerful attitude at work. I took on every duty and assignment with enthusiasm and a can-do attitude. I actively looked for answers rather than wallowing in issues, which helped me get over challenges and keep a dynamic workplace.

Being Well Groomed

Although technical skills are essential in the IT field, I recognized the importance of presenting oneself professionally. I maintained a neat and professional appearance, adhering to the dress code and grooming standards of the organization. By being well groomed, I projected a positive image and represented the organization effectively.

Being Cooperative

Collaboration and cooperation were key during my OJT experience. I actively participated in team discussions, shared knowledge and ideas, and supported my colleagues. I recognized the importance of collective success and fostered a spirit of cooperation to achieve shared objectives.

Being Self-motivated

In the fast-paced environment of my OJT, it was essential to be self-motivated. I consistently set goals for myself, sought out learning opportunities, and pushed myself to go above and beyond the requirements. My self-motivation allowed me to take on additional responsibilities and achieve personal and professional growth.

Demonstrating Good Time Management

Effective time management was crucial during my OJT to meet deadlines and deliver quality work. I organized my tasks, prioritized them based on urgency and importance, and utilized tools like calendars and to-do lists. By effectively managing my time, I was able to maximize productivity and ensure timely completion of assignments.
